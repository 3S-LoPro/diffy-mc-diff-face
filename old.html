What does a Home Warranty Cover in Brookhaven?

A home warranty plan in Brookhaven is put in place to protect against normal wear and tear of systems and appliances in the home. When you have a problem with a home system or appliance that needs to be repaired or replaced due to normal wear and tear, the home warranty will help you pay for it. Each individual covered appliance or system in your plan will have its own details about the limits of the coverage.

There are several home systems that are typically covered under a home warranty, like:

  • Plumbing system
  • Electrical system
  • Water heater
  • HVAC (heating and air conditioning)

Common appliances include:

  • Dishwasher
  • Built-in Microwave
  • Clothes Washer
  • Oven / Range / Stove
  • Clothes Dryer
  • Refrigerator

Is a Home Warranty Worth It in Brookhaven?

Having a home warranty will help to ensure that your budget and your home is properly protected if an issue comes up. Homeownership is a complicated thing, and ensuring that you have all of the information you need before purchasing a home warranty plan is important, which is why we're going to discuss some of the pros and cons of a home warranty. Let's look at the benefits associated with a home warranty first:

  • Peace of mind--Having a home warranty in place means that you can have peace of mind about the costs of repairs. With a home warranty, you know that the fix is just going to be a service fee, which can help reduce your homeowner worries.
  • Workmanship guarantee--A workmanship guarantee ensures that in case a repair does not work, the home warranty company will continue to help you find a solution. Without a home warranty policy, you may pay for the repair and then never hear from that contractor again.
  • Fast response times--It can be a lot of work to get a repairperson you found on your own to give you a fast response. Your home warranty provider generally assures service within a specific period of time, allowing homeowners to get things done quickly and move on.

There are also a few cons of home warranties, such as:

  • Choosing a contractor--You may not have the ability to pick your own contractor based on which home warranty company you choose to go with.
  • Waiting period--If you want to submit a claim right after buying the home warranty policy, that typically isn't possible. Most home warranties have you wait 30 days before you can request service.

Even though you have to pay a premium and service fee for a home warranty, you can still save a good amount of money even if you only make one service call a year. For instance, the average cost of a drain pipe repair is $560, so if you paid a $400 premium for the year and a $100 service fee, that leaves you with $60 in savings for that one call alone.

FAQ's for Home Warranties in Brookhaven

What's the best home warranty company in Brookhaven?

Our overall pick for the best home warranty in Brookhaven is Choice Home Warranty. However, the best company for you depends on what kind of coverage you need for your appliances and home systems.

How is a home warranty different from homeowners insurance?

A home warranty plan protects against the normal wear of systems and appliances in a home. Homeowners insurance protects your home in case of something like a natural disaster or fire. Under a homeowners insurance plan, you will not be able to file a claim should you need a new washing machine or air conditioner, unless the damage is due to a natural disaster.

What kinds of things does a home warranty usually cover?

Your home systems and appliances are covered under a home warranty in Brookhaven. The coverage you choose will affect which systems and appliances are covered, but everything from your stove to your washer can be covered under a home warranty.

Can you use a home warranty immediately after purchasing it?

Most home warranty companies have a 30-day waiting period before you can file claims, but some may offer coverage sooner. Make sure you check with the company about this if quicker coverage is a concern for you.
